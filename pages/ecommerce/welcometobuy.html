Kikaboni Coffee — Rooted in Kenya

“Kikaboni” means organic — and that’s exactly what our coffee is. Grown sustainably in the lush highlands of Kenya, Kikaboni represents a blend of heritage, purity, and community.

Every bean tells a story of resilience and passion. From farmer to roaster, we nurture every step of the journey with care and love for the land.

At Mulinga Connect, we partner directly with smallholder farmers to ensure they earn fair prices and gain global market access — all through transparent, digital channels.

From the red soils of Mt. Kenya to the lush fields of Bungoma, our farmers cultivate coffee with deep passion and generational knowledge.
